About

Rooted in
the music.

Bhoomi — meaning earth in Sanskrit — is an independent record label built for artists who don't fit neatly into any box. We believe music grows best when it's given room to develop in the dark, unhurried, on its own terms.

We work with composers and performers who blend genres, cross languages, and refuse to make the obvious thing. Our artists range from ambient electronic to experimental folk, connected by a commitment to craft and a willingness to go somewhere unexpected.

We handle the legal, business, and creative sides so our artists can focus entirely on the work.
